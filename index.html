About

Born in 1969 in Namrup, Assam, Amitasana Dasa earned a B.Tech in Computer Science from REC, Kurukshetra, and served as a software engineer before dedicating himself to ISKCON in 1992.

He currently holds key leadership roles in Mumbai, Jaipur, and Maharashtra, guiding spiritual programs, youth workshops, and philanthropic initiatives.
