About

Born in 1969 in Namrup, Assam, Amitasana Dasa earned a B.Tech in Computer Science and served as a software engineer before dedicating himself to ISKCON in 1992.

He leads spiritual programs, youth workshops, and philanthropic initiatives in Mumbai, Jaipur, and Maharashtra.
